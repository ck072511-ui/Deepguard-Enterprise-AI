02 · Solution

Why Vision Transformers?

❌ CNN Limitations

  • Local receptive field — misses global inconsistencies
  • Poor generalization across GAN architectures
  • No built-in global context modeling
  • Texture-biased — learns shortcuts

✅ ViT Advantages

  • Self-attention spans the entire image globally
  • Patch-level inconsistency detection
  • ImageNet-21k pre-training → rich representations
  • Attention rollout gives free interpretability
Key Insight: Deepfakes have patch-level boundary artifacts at GAN composition regions — exactly what ViT's patch attention detects best.

05 · Explainable AI

Every Prediction is Explainable

📡 GradCAM

Gradient-weighted class activation maps. Hooks on final transformer block. Highlights why the model made its decision.

α_k = Σ ∂y_c/∂A_kij
L = ReLU(Σ α_k A_k)

👁️ Attention Rollout

Propagates attention through all transformer layers. Shows where the model focused across the entire face.

R = Ā¹ · Ā² · ... · Ā^L
(each Ā^l = 0.5·A^l + 0.5·I)

🔥 Heatmap Overlay

Jet colormap blended onto face crop. Red = suspicious, Blue = authentic.

overlay = cv2.addWeighted(
  face, 0.4, heatmap, 0.6, 0)
